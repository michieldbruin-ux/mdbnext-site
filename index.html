Why work with me

A channel decision, de-risked

Most companies face the same question: build the channel in-house, hire for it, or bring in help. A focused engagement lets you get the direction right and the motion moving before you commit to permanent headcount.

  • 01 Clarity before commitment. Which partner types fit your product and market, and which to pursue first. A clear target beats a strong hire starting from a blank page.
  • 02 The right help for the phase. Setting strategy and running the channel day to day are different jobs. I can do either: a roadmap your team executes, or hands-on ownership for a defined period.
  • 03 Momentum without the wait. Recruiting a permanent lead takes months. An engagement gets the channel moving now, so the eventual hire walks into something that already works.
